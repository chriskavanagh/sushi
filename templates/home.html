Sushi Not Only Tastes Great, It's Great For You! Sushi Is Full Of Nutrients, Including Vitamins And Minerals Such As Iodine Not To Mention Protein. It's Rich In Omega-3 Fatty Acids Which Have Many Amazing Health Benefits. Even The Condiments Are Good For You. Soy Sauce Aids In Digestion And Kills Infection Causing Colon Bacillus.
